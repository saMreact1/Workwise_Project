The Career Test is a 15-minute questionnaire that matches your interests, ambitions, background, personality, and workplace preferences with probable occupations using powerful machine learning. After you have completed the survey, the results will provide you an overview of the types of occupations that match your personality and interests, as well as personalized course recommendations.
